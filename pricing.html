Frequently asked questions

What's included in the monthly price? +
Everything you need to run your bot: AI processing (OpenAI), WhatsApp messaging (Twilio), hosting, maintenance, and ongoing improvements to your bot's responses. There are no hidden API costs or per-message surprises — just one monthly fee.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. There are no annual contracts or lock-ins. Cancel anytime and you'll keep access until the end of your billing cycle. The one-time setup fee is non-refundable.
What happens if I go over my message limit? +
Messages above your plan limit are billed at €0.01 per message. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit so there are no surprises.
Do I need to build the bot myself? +
No. Boundbird is a fully managed service. We configure your bot, train it on your business (products, pricing, policies), connect it to WhatsApp, and keep improving it over time. You don't need any technical knowledge.
When will voice bots be available? +
Voice bots are currently in beta. Plans that include voice bots will have access as soon as it's generally available. If you're on the Custom plan, you can join the beta now.
Does Boundbird connect with my ERP or CRM? +
Yes. Our Custom plan includes native integrations with Sage, HubSpot, Salesforce, Holded, SAP, and more. We connect your bot directly to your existing systems so orders, contacts, and data flow automatically.
