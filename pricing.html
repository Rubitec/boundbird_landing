Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial? +
Yes. Every plan comes with a 7-day free trial. You'll need a card to start, but you won't be charged until the trial ends.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. There are no annual contracts or lock-ins. Cancel from your dashboard and you'll keep access until the end of your billing cycle.
Do I need a LinkedIn Premium account? +
No. Boundbird works with any LinkedIn account. A Sales Navigator subscription can help find better prospects, but it's not required.
How does AI lead scoring work? +
Boundbird's AI analyzes each prospect's profile, company data, activity, and hiring signals, then scores them on how closely they match your ideal client profile.
Can I manage outreach for multiple clients? +
The Agency plan supports up to 5 LinkedIn accounts with team collaboration. Need more? Contact us for a custom plan.
